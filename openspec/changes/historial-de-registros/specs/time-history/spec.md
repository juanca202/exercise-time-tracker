## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Show full Time Record history

The system SHALL read and display in the interface the complete history of all stored Time Records. (US-003 AC-001)

#### Scenario: History lists all records

- **WHEN** the user navigates to the Historial de registros screen and Time Records exist
- **THEN** every stored Time Record is shown in the history table

#### Scenario: Empty history

- **WHEN** the user navigates to the Historial de registros screen and no Time Record exists
- **THEN** the system shows an empty-history state instead of table rows

### Requirement: Total time by Task

The system SHALL compute and display the total accumulated time per Task. (US-003 AC-002)

#### Scenario: Task total sums its records

- **WHEN** two or more Time Records exist for the same Task
- **THEN** the total shown for that Task equals the sum of their Durations

#### Scenario: Task with no records

- **WHEN** a Task has no Time Records
- **THEN** its total is zero

### Requirement: Total time by Project

The system SHALL compute and display the total accumulated time per Project, summing the totals of all its Tasks. (US-003 AC-003)

#### Scenario: Project total sums its tasks' totals

- **WHEN** a Project has two or more Tasks each with Time Records
- **THEN** the Project's total equals the sum of all its Tasks' totals

#### Scenario: Project with no time recorded

- **WHEN** a Project's Tasks have no Time Records
- **THEN** the Project's total is zero

### Requirement: Total time by month

The system SHALL compute and display the total accumulated time per month. When a Time Record generated by the timer crosses the midnight of the last day of a month, its full Duration SHALL be counted in the month corresponding to the record's Start Time, without prorating across the two months. (US-003 AC-004)

#### Scenario: Records within a single month

- **WHEN** Time Records exist with dates entirely within one calendar month
- **THEN** that month's total equals the sum of their Durations

#### Scenario: Timer record crosses a month boundary

- **WHEN** a timer-generated Time Record has a Start Time in one month and an End Time in the following month
- **THEN** its full Duration is counted in the Start Time's month, and none of it is counted in the End Time's month

### Requirement: Reports load in under 2 seconds for up to 1,000 records

The visualization of the time reports (by Task, Project and month) SHALL load in under 2 seconds for a volume of up to 1,000 Time Records. (US-003 AC-005)

#### Scenario: Load time within budget

- **WHEN** the Historial de registros screen is opened with 1,000 stored Time Records
- **THEN** the history list and all totals are fully rendered in under 2 seconds

### Requirement: History screen matches the design system

The Historial de registros screen SHALL adhere to the DESIGN.md "Precision Focus" design system. (US-003 AC-006)

#### Scenario: Design tokens applied

- **WHEN** the Historial de registros screen is rendered
- **THEN** color, typography and spacing tokens match DESIGN.md's Precision Focus theme

### Requirement: History screen matches the Figma prototype

The implementation of the Historial de registros screen SHALL be visually faithful (layout, colors, typography, spacing, components) to the referenced high-fidelity Figma prototype. (US-003 AC-007)

#### Scenario: Visual review against Figma

- **WHEN** the implemented Historial de registros screen is compared against the Figma "Historial de registros" frame
- **THEN** layout, colors, typography, spacing and components match
